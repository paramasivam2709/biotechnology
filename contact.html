Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and appointments.

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by calling our main line at +8801616876080, using our online booking system, or visiting any of our locations in person. We recommend booking in advance for specialized services.

Do you accept health insurance?

Yes, we accept most major health insurance plans. Our billing department will help verify your coverage and explain any out-of-pocket costs before your appointment.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring your ID, insurance card, any relevant medical records, and a list of current medications. If you're coming for a specific test, follow any preparation instructions provided.

How long do appointments typically last?

Appointment durations vary by service. Initial consultations are typically 30-45 minutes, while specialized tests may take 1-2 hours. We'll provide estimated times when you book.
